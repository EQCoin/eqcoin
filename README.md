&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;EQcoin is Passport oriented Decentralized Finance (DeFi) ecosystem of the people, by the people, for the people. Just like Bitcoin and Ethereum, EQcoin is open source, decentralized, permissionless, distributed and public shared digital ledger. EQcoin and Passport are the original commodities of EQcoin ecosystem. As rare commodities EQcoin and Passport have their inherent native value and use value  just like gold, platinum and diamonds. EQcoin and Passport are issued and circulated according to the EQC consensus mechanism via the EQC ecosystem based on decentralized finance, everyone can participate in the issuance and circulation of EQcoin and Passport. The evolution of EQcoin is based on EQcoin ecosystem, everyone can improve and perfect EQcoin via EQcoin Improvement Proposal.
